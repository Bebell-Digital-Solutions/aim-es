How the Course is Structured

  • Seven modules you can finish at your own pace.
  • Each module includes short lessons, practical examples, and simple exercises.
  • Quizzes at the end of each module check your understanding before moving on.
  • A final project helps you apply everything to a real scenario.
  • Total course length: about 3 to 4 hours.

Time Expectations

  • Individual lessons take 3 to 6 minutes to read.
  • Full modules take 20 to 30 minutes.
  • The final project takes 30 to 45 minutes to complete.
  • You can finish the full course in one afternoon or spread it across a week.

Tools You Will Use

You will work with simple, accessible tools:

  • Chat based AI assistants such as ChatGPT or an equivalent tool.
  • Short worksheets to help you plan your AI ideas.
  • Optional browser based tools for writing, transcription, and task automation.

No technical setup is required.

Quizzes and Final Project Details

  • Each quiz has 5 to 8 questions.
  • You need at least 70 percent score to unlock the next module.
  • You can retry quizzes as many times as you want.
  • The final project asks you to:
    • Choose a real or hypothetical business
    • Identify AI opportunities
    • Pick tools that fit those needs
    • Create a short plan you can present
  • The project is graded on clarity and practicality, not technical detail.

Getting Started

To begin the course, simply proceed to the first module. You can work through the material at your own pace and return to any section as needed. The quizzes are designed to reinforce your learning, and the final project will help you apply these concepts to your own work context.

If you have any questions during the course, don't hesitate to use the discussion forums or reach out to our support team.
